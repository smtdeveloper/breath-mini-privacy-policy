Breath Mini Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026

Breath Mini is a wellness app designed to help users relax, focus, and build healthy breathing habits. It is not a medical application and does not provide medical advice.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name or email. The app may store preferences locally on your device.

2. Ads

The app may display advertisements. Third-party ad providers may collect anonymized data to improve ad relevance.

3. In-App Purchases

Breath Mini offers a one-time Pro purchase. Payments are handled securely by Apple App Store or Google Play.

4. Notifications

The app may send local notifications (reminders) scheduled by the user.

5. Data Storage

All user settings are stored locally on your device. We do not store personal data on servers.

6. Disclaimer

Breath Mini is not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any disease. Always consult a healthcare professional if needed.
