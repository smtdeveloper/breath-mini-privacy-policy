Breath Mini Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-03-28

Breath Mini (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Overview

Breath Mini is a breathing and relaxation app designed to help users improve focus, relaxation, and well-being. It is not a medical application and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name or email. The app may store app preferences locally on your device and may use analytics or advertising partners as described in the full policy.

3. In-App Purchases

Breath Mini may offer a one-time Pro purchase. Payment processing is handled by Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

4. Notifications

The app may send local notifications (reminders) scheduled on your device.

5. Disclaimer

Breath Mini is a wellness application only. It is not a medical device and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice.
